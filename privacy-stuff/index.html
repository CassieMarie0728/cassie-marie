PRIVACY STUFF Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters. This PRIVACY STUFF explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We've tried to make this as human-readable as possible.

What Information We Collect

Information You Give Us

  • Email address: If you sign up for our email list or download a freebie, we collect your email address. That's it — we don't ask for more than we need.
  • Form submissions: If you fill out a contact form or other form on this site, we collect whatever information you provide in that form.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics data: We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand how people use this site. This may include pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, and general geographic location (country/region level, not your address).
  • Log data: Our hosting provider may collect standard server log data, including IP addresses, browser type, and pages requested. This is standard for all websites.

How We Use Your Information

  • Email list: To send you the content you signed up for — newsletters, resources, and updates. We will never sell your email address. Ever.
  • Analytics: To understand which content is helpful so we can make more of it. We don't track you across the internet or build advertising profiles.
  • Site improvement: To fix bugs, improve performance, and make the site more useful.

COOKIE SHIT

This site may use cookies — small text files stored on your device. For full details on what cookies we use and how to manage them, see our COOKIE SHIT.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Netlify: Our hosting provider. Netlify PRIVACY STUFF
  • Email service provider: For managing our email list and sending newsletters. Your email address is stored with our email provider.
  • Analytics: If we use analytics, we aim to use privacy-respecting options that don't track you across the web.

Email List

If you join our email list:

  • You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • We may send you emails about new content, resources, and occasional recommendations. We won't spam you.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@griefsurvivalblog.com. We'll respond as quickly as we can.

Children's PRIVACY STUFF

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do our best, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this PRIVACY STUFF from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
