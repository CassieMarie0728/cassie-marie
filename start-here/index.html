Just lost someone? Brain not working? Start with these. They're the survival basics — no fluff, no healing journey garbage.

These guides are designed to be read when your brain is running at about 15% capacity. Short paragraphs. Clear steps. No inspirational bullshit. Read them in order or jump to whatever feels most urgent right now.
